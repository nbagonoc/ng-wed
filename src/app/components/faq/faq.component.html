First Time Visitors

For those of you that are visiting CDO for the first time, I hope you all enjoy it. The city centre is not too far from the beaches (about an hour away by car) and it’s about an hour drive to the airport as well.

To get around the city is easy as well as taxi’s are always available and cheaper than Manila. As I mentioned above you can use an app called Grab. You can’t really get lost in the city as Google Maps is available.

For you shoppers, or you just want good aircon, there are three malls in the city centre. Centrio Ayala malls, (our personal favourite!), SM & Lim Ketkai Mall. There are also hotels nearby each one if you wish to stay around the city centre.

If you would like to take a day trip then there is Dahilayan you can go to. This place has zip lines, mini golf, downhill karting etc. It’s an adventure park for everyone to enjoy. You can also stay overnight there. It is about 2-3 hours drive depending on traffic.

Another trip you can do is to a beach called Camiguin Island. You would need about 2 days for this as it takes about 4 hours to get there. You would need to drive to the port and then take a ferry.

I would suggest to google a few places to go to if you’re planning on staying in CDO for a few days.
